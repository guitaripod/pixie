mako

The backend that powers Midgar Corp's apps.

mako stores as little as possible: an anonymous, device-based identity and a credit balance. It brokers AI requests to providers (OpenAI, Google) only to produce the result you asked for, and never uses your content to train models. It does not sell data, show advertising, or use third-party tracking identifiers.

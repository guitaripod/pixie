Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Pixie ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our AI image generation service.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you authenticate using OAuth providers (GitHub, Google, or Apple), we receive your email address and basic profile information.
  • Generated Content: All images you generate are automatically made public in our gallery.
  • Usage Data: We track your API usage, including the number of images generated and credits consumed.

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, and browser information.
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with our service, including features used and generation parameters.
  • Payment Information: When you purchase credits, payment processing is handled by third-party providers (Stripe or cryptocurrency processors).

How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To provide AI image generation services and process your requests.
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and manage your account.
  • Public Gallery: All generated images are automatically shared in our public gallery for others to view and use.
  • Usage Tracking: To monitor service usage and enforce rate limits.
  • Payment Processing: To process credit purchases and manage your account balance.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our service.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Public Sharing

Important: All images you generate using Pixie are automatically made public and shared in our gallery. This means:

  • Anyone can view your generated images
  • Images remain publicly accessible indefinitely
  • Images may be viewed, downloaded, or used by other users

Third-Party Service Providers

We use third-party services that may process your data:

  • OpenAI: For AI image generation processing
  • Cloudflare: For hosting, storage, and content delivery
  • Stripe: For credit/debit card payments
  • Cryptocurrency Processors: For crypto payments
  • OAuth Providers: GitHub, Google, and Apple for authentication

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Data Retention

  • Images: All generated images are retained indefinitely in our public gallery.
  • Account Data: Your account information is retained while your account is active.
  • Usage Data: Usage analytics are retained for service improvement and analytics purposes.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can request access to your account data.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data (note: public images cannot be deleted).
  • Correction: You can update your account information through your OAuth provider.
  • Opt-out: You can stop using the service at any time.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • OAuth authentication for secure access
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure payment processing through certified providers
  • Regular security audits and updates

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in various locations worldwide through our use of Cloudflare's global network and other service providers.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
